Our Philosophy: Your Training Partner

Our goal isn't for you to use this app forever. It's for you to use it to learn. See how a 10 mph crosswind *really* affects your shot. Understand the exact penalty of hitting from the rough. As you improve, you'll find yourself relying on it less and less. This tool's ultimate success is when you no longer need it.
